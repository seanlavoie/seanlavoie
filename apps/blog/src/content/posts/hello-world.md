After nine years of meaning to write things down, I finally have a place to put them.

This blog will cover what I actually think about day-to-day: distributed systems, infrastructure, developer
tooling, the occasional opinion on software craft, and whatever I'm building or breaking at the moment.

No newsletter. No comments. Just posts.

## What to expect

- Technical deep-dives on things I'm working through
- Notes on architecture and system design tradeoffs
- Writeups on personal projects
- Occasional meta-commentary on the industry

Short posts, long posts, whatever the topic deserves. No cadence commitments.
